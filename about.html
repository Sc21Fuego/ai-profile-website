Since 2021, I have been working as an IT Professional and Network Engineer, dedicating myself to mastering the complexities of modern network infrastructure. I've had the privilege of working on diverse projects that have strengthened my problem-solving abilities and deepened my understanding of how network infrastructure supports business objectives.

Core Competencies

Throughout my career, I have developed expertise in critical areas of network engineering, focusing on technologies that form the backbone of modern enterprise infrastructure. My approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, ensuring robust and scalable solutions for complex networking challenges.

Educational Foundation

Associate of Applied Science (AAS)

Network Design and Administration

Spokane Community College

My educational journey at Spokane Community College provided me with a solid foundation in network design and administration principles. The comprehensive curriculum covered essential topics including network topology design, protocol analysis, security implementation, and infrastructure management.

This formal education equipped me with not only technical skills but also critical thinking abilities and project management competencies that have proven invaluable in my professional career. The hands-on laboratory experiences and real-world case studies prepared me for the challenges I would face in enterprise networking environments.

Technical Expertise

Cisco Routing & Switching

Extensive experience with Cisco networking equipment, including advanced routing protocols, VLAN configuration, spanning tree protocols, and enterprise-grade switching solutions. Proficient in both IOS and IOS-XE platforms.

Palo Alto Firewall Administration

Specialized in Palo Alto Networks firewall management, including security policy configuration, threat prevention, application control, and advanced security feature implementation for comprehensive network protection.

F5 Viprion Administration

Expert in F5 Viprion load balancing solutions, implementing high-availability configurations, SSL termination, traffic management, and application delivery optimization for enterprise environments.

DNS & DHCP Management

Comprehensive knowledge of Domain Name System and Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol implementation, including advanced DNS configurations, DHCP scope management, and integration with enterprise directory services.

Network Time Protocol (NTP)

Proficient in NTP implementation and management across enterprise networks, ensuring accurate time synchronization for logging, authentication, and compliance requirements across distributed network infrastructure.

Cisco ISE (Identity Services Engine)

Advanced experience with Cisco ISE for network access control, including policy enforcement, guest access management, device profiling, and integration with enterprise authentication systems.

Netbrain Network Documentation

Utilizing Netbrain for comprehensive network mapping, automated documentation, change management, and network troubleshooting to maintain accurate and up-to-date infrastructure documentation.

Network Security & Compliance

Strong foundation in network security principles, compliance frameworks, vulnerability assessment, and security best practices for enterprise network infrastructure protection and risk mitigation.
